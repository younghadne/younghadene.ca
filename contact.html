Booking Info

Collab & Features

Everything about booking, features, and working with Young Hadene.

How can I book Young Hadene for a show or feature?

Email contact@younghadene.ca with your project details, budget, and timeline. Booking inquiries are typically responded to within 48 hours.

How do I contact Young Hadene for collaborations?

Fill out the contact form on this page or email contact@younghadene.ca. Include links to your work and what you're looking to create.

Does Young Hadene offer verses or hooks for other artists?

Yes, Young Hadene is open to featured verses and hooks on a case-by-case basis. Reach out via email with your track and vision for the feature.

How to get a Young Hadene feature on my track?

Send your beat or instrumental along with your vision to contact@younghadene.ca. Include any reference tracks and your budget for the feature.

How to submit beats to Young Hadene?

Beat submissions can be sent to contact@younghadene.ca with the subject line "BEAT SUBMISSION". Include your name, contact info, and a link to your beat catalog.

How do I contact Young Hadene for press or media?

Press and media inquiries should be sent to contact@younghadene.ca with "PRESS" in the subject line. Download the press kit for media assets and bio.
